The Short Version

I started designing as a teenager, sketching not just forms — but the way people live and feel. Over time, design became my way of understanding behaviour, emotion, and everyday experience.

From theatre stages in Tehran to digital health platforms in Vancouver, every project starts with the same question: how can this feel more human?

"Good design is invisible because it fits our needs so well."
— Don Norman
