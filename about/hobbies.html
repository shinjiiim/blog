My Hobbies & Interests

Exploring creativity and passion beyond the professional world

Creative Pursuits

Beyond my professional work in UX/UI design, I'm passionate about various creative activities that help me stay inspired and maintain a balanced life. These hobbies not only bring me joy but also influence my design thinking and problem-solving approaches.

🎨 Visual Arts

I enjoy exploring different forms of visual expression, from traditional painting and sketching to digital art. This hands-on creativity helps me better understand color theory, composition, and visual storytelling - skills that directly translate to my design work.

📚 Reading & Learning

I'm an avid reader with interests spanning psychology, behavioral science, design thinking, and fiction. Books like "Thinking, Fast and Slow" by Daniel Kahneman and "Don't Make Me Think" by Steve Krug have profoundly influenced my approach to UX design.

Current reads: Exploring topics in AI ethics, human-computer interaction, and cultural design patterns.

🎭 Cultural Exploration

Having worked with the Shahrzad Theatre Complex and being deeply interested in cross-cultural design, I love attending cultural events, theatre performances, and art exhibitions. These experiences help me understand diverse perspectives and design for global audiences.

🧘‍♀️ Mindfulness & Well-being

I practice mindfulness and yoga to maintain mental clarity and creative energy. This practice has taught me patience, empathy, and the importance of intentional design - approaching problems with a calm and focused mind.

🌱 Personal Growth

I'm passionate about continuous learning and self-improvement. Whether it's learning new design tools, exploring emerging technologies, or developing soft skills, I believe in staying curious and adaptable.

Currently exploring: Motion design, AI tools for designers, and sustainable design practices.

How Hobbies Influence My Work

  • Enhanced creativity: Diverse interests provide fresh perspectives and inspiration for design solutions
  • Cultural sensitivity: Exposure to different cultures improves my ability to design for diverse users
  • Better problem-solving: Skills from hobbies often translate to innovative approaches in UX challenges
  • Work-life balance: Hobbies help prevent burnout and maintain enthusiasm for design work
