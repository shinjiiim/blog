Downtown Radiology

UX/UI Designer at Wallgate Holdings Inc

Role UX/UI Designer
Company Wallgate Holdings Inc
Timeline 2019 - 2021
Industry Healthcare / Radiology

Project Overview

Designed digital solutions for Downtown Radiology, a medical imaging facility under Wallgate Holdings Inc. Focused on creating user-friendly interfaces for both patients and healthcare professionals to improve the radiology service experience.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Designed patient portal for appointment booking and result viewing
  • Created internal tools for radiologists and administrative staff
  • Conducted user research with patients and healthcare providers
  • Developed design systems for consistent healthcare interfaces
  • Ensured compliance with healthcare privacy regulations (HIPAA)

Key Challenges

  • Designing for diverse user groups (patients, doctors, technicians)
  • Meeting strict healthcare compliance and security requirements
  • Simplifying complex medical imaging workflows
  • Creating accessible interfaces for elderly patients

Solutions & Impact

  • Streamlined appointment booking process, reducing phone calls by 40%
  • Improved patient satisfaction with clear result presentation
  • Enhanced staff efficiency with intuitive internal tools
  • Implemented WCAG AA accessibility standards

Skills Applied

Design: Healthcare UI/UX, Accessibility design, Design systems, Prototyping

Research: User interviews, Usability testing, Workflow analysis

Compliance: HIPAA standards, Healthcare privacy, Data security
