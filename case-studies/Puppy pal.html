BC Children's Hospital Digital Lab

Research and Development Lab Designing Digital Healthcare Applications

Role UX Researcher, UI Designer
Organization BC Children's Hospital / UBC
Timeline 2022 - 2023
Focus Healthcare, Pediatrics

Project Overview

Worked in a research and development lab designing digital applications in collaboration with BC Children's Hospital, funded by UBC University. The lab focused on creating innovative healthcare solutions for pediatric patients and their families.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Conducted user research with patients, families, and healthcare providers
  • Designed healthcare-focused digital applications and interfaces
  • Collaborated with medical professionals to understand clinical workflows
  • Ensured accessibility and usability for diverse user groups
  • Participated in research documentation and academic publications

Key Challenges

  • Designing for sensitive healthcare contexts and vulnerable populations
  • Balancing medical accuracy with user-friendly design
  • Meeting strict healthcare privacy and security requirements
  • Accommodating various literacy levels and age groups

Solutions & Impact

  • Created empathy-driven designs based on extensive user research
  • Developed accessible interfaces compliant with healthcare standards
  • Implemented clear information hierarchy for medical content
  • Designed age-appropriate experiences for pediatric users

Skills Applied

Research: Healthcare user research, Ethnographic studies, Accessibility research

Design: Healthcare UI/UX, Inclusive design, Information architecture

Collaboration: Cross-disciplinary teamwork, Academic research, Clinical stakeholders
