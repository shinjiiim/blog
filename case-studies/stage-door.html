Stage Door Dance

Enhancing User Satisfaction, Usability, and Completion Rate for Dance Studio Website

Role UX/UI Designer, Researcher
Timeline 4 weeks
Tools Figma, Maze, Miri, Mixed method research

Project Overview

Stage Door Dance is a premier dance studio offering classes for all ages and skill levels. The studio needed a website redesign to improve user experience, increase class registrations, and better showcase their vibrant community and talented instructors.
I led the UX redesign of the Stagedoor Dance platform to improve navigation, modernize the experience, and support clearer class discovery and registration for dancers and parents.

  • The previous site had grown through fragmented feature additions, resulting in cluttered layouts, unclear navigation, and inconsistent information structure , leading to confusion and user drop-offs.

My Role & Focus

  • UX Research & Heuristic Evaluation
  • Information Architecture & Card Sorting
  • Wireframing & Interaction Design
  • Prototyping & Usability Testing
  • Visual Refresh & Component Design

The Challenge

The existing website faced several critical issues founded after team sessions heuristic evaluation and in depth interviewing with users:

  • High bounce rate (68%) and low completion rate for class registration
  • Confusing navigation structure making it difficult to find class schedules
  • Poor mobile experience despite 65% of traffic coming from mobile devices
  • Outdated visual design that didn't reflect the studio's energetic brand
  • No clear call-to-action for trial class sign-ups
